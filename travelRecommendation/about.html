About Us

Welcome to MiHoYo-TravelBloom!

MiHoYo is a global interactive entertainment company known for creating immersive games, including “Genshin Impact,” “Honkai Impact 3rd,” and “Tears of Themis.”

We focus on innovation and storytelling, combining cutting-edge technology with creative passion to deliver engaging experiences to players worldwide. MiHoYo is dedicated to inspiring players through our “tech otakus save the world” philosophy.

Our values include integrity, innovation, customer satisfaction, and teamwork. We believe in putting our customers first and working together to achieve our goals. Feel free to explore our website to learn more about what we offer.
